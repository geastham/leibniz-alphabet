The PROPOSER suggested **infinity** - the unbounded, endless.

The CRITIC found a derivation:
- **Infinity = Negation(Existence(Quantity-Maximum))**

**Verdict**: REJECT with 0.85 confidence

**Key Insight**: Infinity is unbounded quantity. Just as continuity = no smallest part, infinity = no largest quantity.
