## The Candidates

### 1. QUANTITY (ACCEPTED)

The PROPOSER filled the "how much" gap with **quantity** - the measurable magnitude of things.

The CRITIC confirmed its primitiveness:
- **Decomposition**: "Amount", "number", "measure" all ARE quantity
- **Orthogonality**: Quality = WHAT KIND, Quantity = HOW MUCH - truly independent dimensions

**Verdict**: ACCEPT with 0.87 confidence

**Key Insight**: Quality and quantity form a complete pair for characterizing things. Together they answer the fundamental questions: what kind of thing, and how much of it?

### 2. AGENCY (REJECTED)

The PROPOSER suggested **agency** - the capacity to initiate action.

The CRITIC found a decomposition:
- **Agency = Intentional Causation**
- An agent is a cause whose efficacy is directed by intentional states
- Since we have intentionality (P007) and can derive causation, agency is expressible!

**Verdict**: REJECT with 0.72 confidence

**Key Insight**: This rejection demonstrates the POWER of our primitives! Agency seemed fundamental but can be constructed from intentionality + causation. The alphabet is more expressive than it appeared.

### 3. COMPOSITION (ACCEPTED)

The PROPOSER suggested **composition** - the part-whole relation.

The CRITIC found it irreducible and meta-essential:
- **Decomposition**: Set membership fails (my hand is part of me, not a member of {me})
- **Meta-essential**: The alphabet itself works by composition - complex concepts are COMPOSED of primitives!

**Verdict**: ACCEPT with 0.85 confidence

**Key Insight**: Composition is uniquely self-referential in our alphabet. It's the primitive that explains how ALL other primitives combine. The prime product scheme IS compositional mereology.
